Board:

- vector<vector<HexTile>> (the board is a "matrix" where the first line is the first hex line and the first column is made of
the leftmost hex tiles of the board)

HexTile:

- a HexTile contains useful info for the game like the color of the tile, the number of white pieces, black pieces, who controls the hex and all of its 6 neihgbours

Action:

- an action is defined by a `from` tile, a `to` tile, the number of white pieces moved, black pieces moved, wheter is an Imprisonment
or capture and other potentially useful info like the direction of the movement and the steps

Game:

- a game contains info like the board, the 2 players, whose player turn is, the number of turns played so far, the last turn a caputure or imprisonment was made, who the winner is and whether the game is over
